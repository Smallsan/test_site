Meet Our Founder & CEO

Christian Tan - Founder & CEO

With over 20 years of experience as a professional photographer and 10 years in the camera retail industry, Marcus Rodriguez founded Protograph with a vision to create the world's most knowledgeable and customer-focused camera store. A graduate of the Rochester Institute of Technology's School of Photographic Arts and Sciences, Marcus brings both technical expertise and artistic vision to Protograph.

"Photography is about capturing emotion, telling stories, and preserving memories," says Marcus. "The camera is just a tool, but the right tool in the right hands can create magic. At Protograph, we help photographers find their perfect creative partner."

Before founding Protograph, Marcus worked as a wedding and portrait photographer for over a decade, capturing over 1,000 weddings and countless family portraits. This real-world experience gives him unique insight into what photographers actually need from their camera systems. When not leading the company, Marcus continues to shoot professionally and teaches photography workshops at local art schools.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted authority on mirrorless camera technology, where professional expertise meets passionate service, empowering photographers to capture their vision and tell their stories through the transformative power of cutting-edge camera systems.
